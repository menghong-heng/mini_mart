NOTES
-----

- Staff passwords are stored in Oracle as MD5 hashes for demo purposes.
- Staff accounts are seeded by data/seed.sql on a fresh database.
- Staff accounts can be synced into an existing database with:
  docker compose exec backend python manage_staff.py
- Customer accounts are separate from staff accounts and use separate tables.
